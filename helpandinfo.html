Helpful Information

If you think you suffer from any of the aforementioned anxiety disorders, please attend your GP. Upon examination of your symptoms, they will provide a refferal to a qualified therapist for treatment.
For more information on symptoms of Anxiety Disorders, please visit hse.ie/anxiety
For more information on symptoms of OCD, please visit hse.ie/ocd




The following are some external links to some very helpful websites:

  1. The Feeling Good Website by Dr. David Burns
  2. For OCD sufferers, see Jon Hershfield, MFT






List of recommended self-help books for anxiety disorders.

  • Feeling Great, The Revolutionary New Treatment for Depression and Anxiety, by Dr. David Burns
  • 10 Days to Great Self-Esteem, by Dr. David Burns
  • When Panic Attacks, by Dr. David Burns
  • Everyday Mindfulness for OCD, by Jon Hershfield, MFT
  • The Mindfulness Workbook for OCD, by Jon Hershfield, MFT

Depending on the severity of the disorder, the self-help books will not work on their own. CBT Therapy is highly recommended.
